Science fiction art has a rich history, evolving from early illustrations in the works of authors like Jules Verne and H.G. Wells to the vibrant pulp magazine covers of the 20th century. It has become a diverse genre that spans digital art, film, and gaming, visualizing the boundless possibilities of the future.

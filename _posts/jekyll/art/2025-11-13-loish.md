Lois van Baarle, known online as Loish, is a digital artist and character designer from the Netherlands. Her art is known for its vibrant colors and expressive characters. She has worked with clients like LEGO, Amazon, and Guerrilla Games, and has published three artbooks.

Loish is also passionate about sharing her knowledge and has a large following on social media. She offers tutorials and resources for aspiring artists on her website.

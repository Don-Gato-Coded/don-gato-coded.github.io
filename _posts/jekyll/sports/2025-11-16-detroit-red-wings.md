The Detroit Red Wings, one of the "Original Six" NHL teams, were founded in 1926. Originally the Detroit Cougars, they became the Falcons in 1930 and finally the Red Wings in 1932. The team has a storied history, winning 11 Stanley Cup championships, the most of any U.S.-based NHL franchise. Key eras of success include the 1950s with Gordie Howe and the late 1990s-2000s with stars like Steve Yzerman and Nicklas Lidstrom.

The Michigan Wolverines, representing the University of Michigan, have a storied history in collegiate athletics. The football program, established in 1879, holds the record for the most all-time wins in college football history and claims 12 national titles. The men's basketball team won the NCAA championship in 1989 and has made eight Final Four appearances. Across all sports, Michigan has secured over 55 national championships.
